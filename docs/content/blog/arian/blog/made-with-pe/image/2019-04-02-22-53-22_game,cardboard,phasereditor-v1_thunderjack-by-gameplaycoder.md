Thunderjack! A game made by the [GamePlayCoder.com](https://gameplaycoder.com/2019/02/10/thunderjack-html5-game-update-a-little-more-polish/) is a nice example of what you can do with Phaser Editor. It is available in the Play Store and you can download the source code from [GitHub](https://github.com/Cartrell/ThunderjackHtml5).

In addition, the author posts about the details of the games in his [blog](https://gameplaycoder.com/blog).
