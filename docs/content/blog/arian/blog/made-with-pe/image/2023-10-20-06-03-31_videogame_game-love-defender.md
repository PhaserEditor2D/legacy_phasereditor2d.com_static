[Play it here](https://www.iololoi.com/#love-defender)

Hardcore fast 2D shoot 'em up platformer. This game is too hard and unfair for you, Don't play it or it will make you cry and break your controller in anger. Your life won't be the same.

![screenshot][2]

You are Jerry Fletcher - the last line of defense against extraterrestrial forces - a fighter jet tasked with stopping alien invaders from an unknown part of the universe threatening your world of Love. 

![screenshot][3]

The invaders already infiltrated other neighboring factions, disrupting the delicate balance of power. Welcome to "Love Defender" blend of shoot 'em up and platformer gameplay. Yes, you read that right—it's a unique mix. Fast-paced, merciless, and maybe even a bit unfair, you'll need to annihilate enemy forces in every mission, rescue innocent civilians, and face off against alien possesed boss creatures at the end of each world. 

![screenshot][4]

Journey through diverse realms: your home base, the world of brutalism, the jungle, vast desert, and the lunar nazi base. In each, battle distinctive enemies representative of their factions. 

![screenshot][5]

A dash of investigation and puzzle-solving might aid you, but your primary assets are your sharpshooting and hand-to-hand combat skills. Soar with precision, react rapidly, and grab every power-up your allies drop your way. Don't forget to blast those barrels for some unexpected bonuses. With "Love Defender", it's all about speed and strategy. Dive in, take charge, and let glory be yours.

Android, Linux, Win & Apple (not compatible with safari)
