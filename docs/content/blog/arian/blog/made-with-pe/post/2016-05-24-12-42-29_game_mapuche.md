[Mapuche][1] is a board game for kids, to motivate them to get closer with the nature.

This game was made in the [Global Game Jam 2016 (Cuba)][2] by the [Globex team][3] of the [University of the Informatics Sciences (UCI)][4].

![Globex][6]
