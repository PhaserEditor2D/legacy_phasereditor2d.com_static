Hi!

In these years, many of you requested discounts for purchasing a couple of licenses for a team of developers. It was an informal way of support teams.

Today we are formalizing a new type of license for small teams, the **Phaser Editor Team License**:

* Can be shared by 5 developers
* Is valid only for two years
* It has a price of $125

When you purchase a **Phaser Editor Team License**, a license key is sent to your email. You can share that key with your team of 5 (or less) developers. Each developer can activate an instance of Phaser Editor 2D in its computer (at the office and home).

If your team is bigger, you can contact us at `developers@phasereditor2d.com`.

Now, [get your team license](https://gumroad.com/l/phasereditor)!
