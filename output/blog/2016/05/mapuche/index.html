Mapuche

May 24, 2016 - Posted in made-with-pe

Mapuche is a board game for kids, to motivate them to get closer with the nature.

This game was made in the Global Game Jam 2016 (Cuba) by the Globex team of the University of the Informatics Sciences (UCI).

In this event Grethel Naranjo introduced Phaser and we was invited to present Phaser Editor.
