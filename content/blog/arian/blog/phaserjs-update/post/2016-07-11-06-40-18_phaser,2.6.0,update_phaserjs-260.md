Hello,

[Phaser v2.6.0 is released][1] and you can update Phaser Editor to support it.

Check the [PhaserJS for Phaser Editor][2] repository for detailed instructions, or [this tutorial][3].

The process is simple, in general it is just about to download the metadata repository and link it on the editor.

If you have any problem please [add an issue][4] or contact with us in any of our social channels.
